Arts & Crafts

A journey through the living heritage of Kutch

Handcrafted Legacies

Kutch is renowned worldwide for its rich diversity of handicrafts. Each community has its own distinct tradition, passed down through generations, making the region a living museum of textile and craft art.

Block Printing

Ajrakh

Ancient geometric block printing using natural dyes like indigo and madder.
Tie-Dye

Bandhani

Intricate tie-dye technique creating beautiful dotted patterns on silk and cotton.
Painting

Rogan Art

Rare oil-based painting on fabric, practiced by a single family in Nirona.

Textile

Kutch Weaving

Vibrant handloom weaving known for its intricate extra-weft designs.
Ceramics

Khavda Pottery

Distinctive painted pottery from Northern Kutch using local clay and natural colors.
Leatherwork

Leather Art

Handcrafted leather goods decorated with threadwork and traditional motifs.
Wall Art

Lippan Kaam

Traditional mud and mirror work that adorns the walls of Bhungas.
Metalwork

Sudi & Chappu

The art of crafting sharp, embellished knives and nutcrackers.
