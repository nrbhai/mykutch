About Us


MyKutch — celebrating people, craft and landscape

We are a community-rooted travel and storytelling project focused on Kutch. We design low-impact journeys, support artisans, and publish stories that preserve and share local knowledge.

Plan a trip Contact the team

Who we are

Founded by locals and travellers who love Kutch, MyKutch is built to celebrate everyday culture — from mud homes and crafts to salt flats and festivals. We partner with village guides and artisans to ensure benefits flow to local communities.

Our approach

  • Community-led: Tours and experiences are co-created with local hosts.
  • Craft preservation: We promote makers and help connect them with markets.
  • Sustainability: Low-impact itineraries, local procurement and fair pricing.

Impact

We measure impact by earnings for artisans, village partnerships, and conservation outcomes — not just visitor numbers. Our micro-grants and training programs have helped artisans gain sustainable orders and visibility.

Why Kutch matters

Kutch is a mosaic of ecosystems and cultures — salt marshes, deserts, fishing hamlets and textile villages. Supporting local livelihoods and crafts keeps these traditions alive and provides meaningful experiences for visitors who want to learn and contribute.
