Madhapar — India's Richest Village

Located just 3 km from Bhuj, Madhapar is globally renowned as the "Richest Village in India." This unique town redefines the concept of a "village" with its prosperity, housing nearly 20 bank branches holding deposits worth over ₹5,000 Crores. Home to a prosperous Patel community with a vast NRI population residing in the UK, USA, Africa, and Australia, Madhapar boasts grand bungalows, luxury amenities, and a lifestyle that rivals major cities while retaining its strong cultural roots.

How to Reach

  • By Road: Just a 10-15 minute drive (4 km) from Bhuj city center on the Bhuj-Anjar highway.
  • From Airport/Station: Very accessible from Bhuj Airport and Railway Station via auto-rickshaw or taxi.
  • Local Transport: Frequent shared autos (Chhakdas) and buses run between Bhuj and Madhapar.
