HiraLaxmi Memorial Park

Located in Bhujodi, the **Vande Mataram Memorial** is a grand tribute to India's freedom struggle, celebrated as India's first 4D memorial. The main structure is an architecturally stunning, larger-than-life replica of the **Indian Parliament Building (Sansad Bhavan)**. Spanning over 12 acres, this immersive museum uses cutting-edge 4D technology in 17 different rooms to transport visitors back in time, reliving key moments of the independence movement from the Revolt of 1857 to 1947. It is an initiative of the Ashapura Foundation and stands as a beacon of patriotism and history.

Why Visit

  • Immersive History: Experience the freedom struggle like never before with life-like 4D exhibits and animatronics.
  • Architectural Grandeur: Walk through a massive replica of the Indian Parliament and see the 30-foot Bharat Mata statue.
  • Family Entertainment: A perfect blend of education and technology that engages both children and adults.
  • Night Spectacle: The mesmerizing light and sound "Facade Show" on the Parliament building happens every evening.

Visitor Info

  • Timings: 11:00 AM to 7:30 PM (Museum). Closed on Mondays.
  • Facade Show: Starts after dusk (approx 8:00 PM). magnificent light projections.
  • Entry Fee: Ticketed entry for Museum and Show. (Check counter format current rates).
  • Facilities: Cafeteria, Library, and ample parking space available onsite.

Key Attractions

Highlights within the complex

Main Museum (Sansad Bhavan)

The centerpiece of the memorial. Inside, 17 galleries use 4D effects to depict episodes like the Dandi March and 1857 Revolt.

Bharat Mata Statue

A colossal 30-foot tall statue of Bharat Mata weighing 30 tons, standing as a symbol of national pride.

Facade Light & Sound Show

An open-air night show using high-end projection mapping on the building's facade to narrate India's journey.

Hiralaxmi Craft Park

Located within the same complex, a vibrant space to explore and buy traditional Kutchi handicrafts directly from artisans.

Curated Local Advice

Combine Visits

Plan to visit Bhujodi weavers in the afternoon (3-5 PM), then Hiralaxmi Craft Park, and end with the Memorial Museum and night show.

Time Required

You need at least 2-3 hours to do justice to the Museum and Craft Park. Arrive by 4:00 PM for a relaxed tour.
