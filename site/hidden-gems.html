Nature & Wildlife

Chaari Dhand

A seasonal wetland sanctuary that transforms into a haven for migratory birds during winter. Witness thousands of flamingos, cranes, and other exotic species in their natural habitat.

🦩 Bird Sanctuary 🌤️ Oct - Mar

Highlights

  • ✓ Over 75 species of migratory birds
  • ✓ Flamingo colonies in winter
  • ✓ Peaceful sunrise photography
  • ✓ Local wildlife sightings

How to Reach

Located 60 km from Bhuj. Best visited with a local guide. Carry binoculars and camera gear for the best experience.

Heritage & History

Kotay Sun Temple

Ancient temple ruins dating back to the Solanki dynasty, featuring exquisite stone carvings and architectural marvels. A testament to Kutch's rich historical heritage.

🏛️ Ancient Temple 🌤️ Year Round

Highlights

  • ✓ 12th century architecture
  • ✓ Intricate stone carvings
  • ✓ Peaceful spiritual atmosphere
  • ✓ Stunning sunset views

How to Reach

Located near Kotay village, 35 km from Bhuj. Accessible by road. Best visited in morning or evening light for photography.
